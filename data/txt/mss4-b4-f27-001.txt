Dear Elizabeth
I am very sorry to hear
of my Mother's illness I hope she is better
this eve. Betsy brought our little one to see
us today I hope they were home before the
shower. Please to send me word how
mother is by Johnny Lenner tomorrow
letter she will be able to come [deletion]tomorrow[/deletion]
Eben will show thee how the lock is fastened
next first day. I cannot go in tomorrow
as Eben will be sending to Carlow
Mick forgot the tongue & gall he was
bringing out.
Please to send Sally to Johnny Lenner
for the spelling book I desired him to get &
1824
thine D.P.

E Leadbeater
