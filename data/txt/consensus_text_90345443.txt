1st. Tuesday. We had better
accounts of Liddy who however
is not recovering as fast as
we could wish.
Papa returned this evening
having slept in town last night.
2 Wednesday. N P.
3 Thursday N. P.
4 Friday Papa did not go
to town to day.
5 Saturday. A more cheerful
account from Liddy who is
now able to be down with the
other girls. Papa had also
a very pleasant letter from
Avy who says he enjoyed himself
to the "highest pitch" during the
Easter holy days.
6 Sunday. Went to meeting with
Papa 'Mama' & Ned & saw Florence
April [unclear][/unclear].
