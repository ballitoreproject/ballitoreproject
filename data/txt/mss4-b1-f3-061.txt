R. S. to his Daughter G.

					Ballitore, 18th of Seventhmonth 1778
		** Thy account of thy visit to the Widow R. was very agreeable. The sick room is often a profitable place. When the favoured of Heaven are in the furnace of affliction, the Son, the Comforter, is often sensibly among them; and where he is, it is good for us to be ***
						R. S.
