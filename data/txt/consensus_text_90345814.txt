28th Sunday - Papa, Ned, Lyd and I
went to meeting - As Grandmama
invited us to stay and dine with her
we did so - except Ned who walked
home to tell them that we were staying.
Our three Uncles - Aunt Martha and
Frank were there - and a [underline]very[/underline]
pleasant evening we had.
29th.. Monday - N. P. except that
it was very fine and mild -
30th.. Tuesday - The last day of November
Tempus fugit - Christmas is
not far off now -
[underline]November[/underline]
