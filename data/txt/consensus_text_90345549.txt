When in the drawing room after
tea Mr Barter brought some of his
beautiful medallions to show me - likenesses
carved in walrus ivory with a carnelian
for ground. They were brooch size
and uncommonly pretty - He said
he made one before dinner which he
had begun in the morning - quick
work certainly - ! Tomorrow
I am to have a glimpse into his
study -
