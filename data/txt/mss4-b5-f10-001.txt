If miss Mary Shackleton will
be so obliging as to greet Mrs. Baxter
at Timalin about 3 a clock on Monday
next with old Headq & ye Carr it will
be look'd on as a pticular favor as
it will give Mrs. Baxter an oppertunity
to see her good friends at Balitore which
the could not otherways have as there
are more company in ye. Carriage who
phaps wou'd not chuse to go aff ye road
10 atd. July 3d
1778

Miss Mary Shackleton
Ballitore
S Robt.
S. Carlow
Mary Baxter
Miss Mary Shackleton
Balitore
