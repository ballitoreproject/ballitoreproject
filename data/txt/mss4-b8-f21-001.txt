Mervill May 28 1822
Sir
I am Very Sorry to be obliged to trouble
you oncrd. more but the times is a little
pinching or else would not and if you would be
So Kind as to Send me by the Bearer - one Hundred
of Oatmeal and a sack of potatoes. to put me over
the Sumer - i Should never trouble your goodness any
more and by So doing you would much oblige yours
Anastatia Butler and
Michael [underline]Flinn[/underline]

to my mind. for fraid of Freting her
my Situation. Then she bed me Appeal to you
