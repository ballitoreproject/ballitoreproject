14th of 10th mo. 1826 -
My dear E - I send the knives by
R Davis who goes to see the Doctor
Alas I am sorry to hear he is so
poorly, I hope I sent thee the
corks which cost 3/- knives 4/-
was 8 7..8 so that I am in thy
debt 2..4 still - G Downes &
family have arrived safely & are to
dine with us to day along with Mary
who is in good spirits SB.

Elizth Leadbeater
Ballitore
