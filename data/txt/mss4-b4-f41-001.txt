My dear Grandmother
good jar raisins from Waterford I thought they
would be good for & agreeable to thee so hope
thou wilt accept them from
Clonmel 12th Mo 8th 1795
As my father has got some
thy affect Grand-daughter
Anne Grubb
Anne Grubb
To
Elizabeth Shackleton
[underline]Ballitore[/underline]

Anne Grubb
Clonmel. 8/12 mo: 1795.
