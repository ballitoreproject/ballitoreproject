6th Mo, 1827
My dear E - I did get good accounts from
E & expect to see him in a day or two
I hear Jas Webb is much better & that R &
I need not have hurried home I intend
taking [unclear]Meg[/unclear] Newman thy letter this eve & pay by
Geroge 4 thou sent too much calico
there were many thanks to thee for thy
various presents I may add mine &
am sorry I have nothing to put in the basket
which I return along with one of D's
to whom give my dear love it is a shame
for me not to have a letter written for
her ready for G's man but I have been
very busy with stitching to make the
children & window look decent
for Edw I have a basket of D's still in town
a very old one, I have not seen the Hopkinses
since, all I am sorry for is that thy day
was so very short however it may make
an open for thee to fly up again some-
other time Emily is here still & sends her
love I am provoked with myself for
not having a letter written to thee sooner
that I need not now hurry so much
I got thy 1 [unclear][/unclear] & J B's bill

George is extremely kind tell D
to send me as little of the check &
calico as she can as I think that
must be a great deal [deletion]she[/deletion] we have got so
much among us, I fear Anne Toole
will not suit Jane & I hope M Wallace
will suit us I think she will do best for
the children she washes well but cooks
badly I hope nothing may prevent
my going after the wedding to see you
as the little [underline]taste[/underline] I had of thee only makes
me long for more, ah why did thou
send asparragus? surely it is a great deal
too nice for me Johnny sends his love to
thee, we miss'd thee greatly M Dawson paid
her little visit on Dublin & was hard set to
get away, I hope soon to look after the [unclear]china[/unclear]
never fear I will not fatigue myself about
it I am loth to keep the hamper, for it if
[unclear][/unclear] be done with it as it wd be hard to get
it to town & I have a basket there which
will do give my dear love to my father
I am afraid to add more lest I should
miss the opportunity thy SB
Meg sends her love
