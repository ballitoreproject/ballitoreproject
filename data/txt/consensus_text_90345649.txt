28 Tuesday - To meeting again
and an immense long one it
was - 4 hours! but we did not
stay for all of it - called at
Aunt Cole's and then home -
Elizabeth Fayle - (Susan Evan's
grandmother) and daughter,
with two other young women
dined - Polly and Thomas
went to Mary Edmondson's -
Caroline and Lucy Pim called
after dinner - to see Polly and
her baby -. Richard Shackleton
to tea -. Polly & Lyd went to
Aunt Cole's but I came out
with Papa by 8 O'clock train,
to let Huldah in for a few days
29 Wednesday - She and Papa
drove in with Ned also -.
The latter returned this evening,
