Polly, Lilly, and I called
to see Aunt Martha who
happened for a wonder
to be at home, and who
gave us a hearty welcome.
She, Uncle Manliffe, &
Uncle Arthur, have not
long returned from a
yachting trip which
they seem to have enjoyed,
They spent two days at
Ballievey I believe and
while up near Warren
Point met the Whites.
and there Pic nic party.
16 ladies! and but 12
gentlemen I hear - a sad
proportion. Well!
when at Glendruid Uncle
Manliffe visited us all
