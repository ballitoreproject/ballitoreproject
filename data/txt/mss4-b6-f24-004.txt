January the 8 1811 -
Der. Madam [deletion]yo[/deletion] thees Derictions has out
Done our Doctors in our Country for this
Woman that thees Derections has taken
thee are getting quite well for the skin
on her was so stret that thee was as hard
as stone and now: thee are in spots quite
slack and other places of thee in welts
Middleing hard and in one side of thee Belly
are splen when thee stir or Cof but Cofs but
Seldom and when thee swallow thee spittle
thee finds thee Hart as Cold as stone -

To Mrs. Lead
Beater Ballatore
