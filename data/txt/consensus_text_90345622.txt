6th.. Tuesday - Cold gloomy day -
Walk with Ned to the Paper mill and
back. Turkish bath with Miss [unclear]Hannay[/unclear]
and Mrs Disney -
Dinner time. Sitting round
the fire after it - Walk after dinner
with --, Tea time - Ned
staid down longer than usual - Miss
Hannay sang for us - Conversation
after it with -- The new
comer is a Mr Todd and I believe
one of the firm Todd and Barns
Ned talks of going tomorrow so
perhaps this will be my last night
at Blarney - It is now about
11½ and here I am up in my room
scribbling away - Oh dear! -
