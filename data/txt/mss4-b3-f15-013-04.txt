Ballitore: 17th of 1st month 1787

My dear friend

++ I wrote to  I did not feel much virtue to proceed with it - but I thought reason and friendship demanded the best I had. I have often begged to be preserved from forward, unwarrantable, voluntary offerings and a breach of the third commandment: at the same time I think it most excellent & desirable to be made the vehicle of unmixed good to any.
+++++++++
Remember me affectionately to dear  I sincerely desire that she may be strengthened in body and mind - out of weakness she has often been made strong, so she has abundant cause still to confide in him who maketh a way for his chosen children, where they can see no way; yea, who hangeth the Earth upon nothing.
