My dear Eliz:
I have sent thy letter to Matty & cannot follow it,
because I have seen Lucy B in a fine measles, & add this
to request from A Widdons that thou will get the money from
E. Taylor for the stockings as the money was not paid in
Dublin.
Thy MLeadbeater
12/-5 mo:

Eliz Leadbeater
J Haughtons'
Carlow
