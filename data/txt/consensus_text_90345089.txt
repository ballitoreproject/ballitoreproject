12th..- We did not go
out this morning till
past twelve when Emmy
and I went up to a field
beyond the thistly brae
from which I sketched a
pretty view, while Emmy
read - Dined at three
Aunt E only taking a little
snack as they intended
to dine at Mrs Welshe's -.;
accordingly they went at
about five- and we three
young people to tea at seven
There were two nieces of Mrs
Welshe's there one married
to a Mr Aitkins I believe who
was there also - and Mr Law
