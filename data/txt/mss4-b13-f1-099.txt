First-day morning M:D bore indeed a notable testimony, she stood near an hour, & I expect had a (in part) clearing out: - shea addressed wisely the ^(many)^ different states in this meeting, amongst many other applicable scriptures this she beautifully enlarged upon, “Awake, awake, put on thy strength, O Zion, put on thy beautiful garments, O Jerusalem the holy city: for henceforth there shall be no more come into thee the uncircumcised, & the unclean. Shake thyself from the dust: arise & sit down, O Jerusalem: loose thyself from the bands of thy neck, O captive daughter of Zion”: In the afternoon meeting Mary stood again forth in the best cause, & we came home to tea: after wch. Robt. Benson read a few beautiful chapters, & a short silent pause ensued. Wilson Birkbeck & another frd.
