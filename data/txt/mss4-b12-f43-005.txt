Carton 8 Novr 2nd 1813
Sir,
I received your letter this morning
I request you will have the goodness
to thank Mrs Leadbeater for her
last Publication which you enclosed.
I am sorry, I had not the pleasure
of meeting you at Ballitore.
I have the honor to be
your obedient servt.
Thos. J. Rawson Esq
Attn.
Leinster
