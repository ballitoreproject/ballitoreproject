11 Monday. This evening Uncle

12 Tuesday. Papa went to town
and brought Margt out with
him in the evening. Charlotte
is in rather a delicate state of
health and we are in hopes the
change of air may be of use to
her -
13 Wednesday - a pouring wet
day until after dinner when
the rain partly ceased, and
the wind became very strong,
but at night it grew calmer,
poor Margt could make little
use of the country air.
14 Thursday. Fine and very mild.
Papa and Margt started for
town after a very early breakfast
