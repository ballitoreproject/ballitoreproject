From Dick to Papa when he was
5 years old.
Edward Barrington
186 Great Britain Street
4th of March 1834
I [insertion]saw[/insertion] The garden; I saw the
Kittens, all the May
bushes. I was helping the
man to dig up the weeds.
I saw the little birds
nest. I like to be here.
I got a little egg that
little Polly Hopkins
laid. I saw the cock
his name is Neddy
Hopkins.
Richard
