Oriel. 10 Dec. [insertion][19][/insertion] 16.
My dear Pilgrim.
A line to say that your
papers satisfy us & we
shall be glad to [unclear]see[/unclear] you a
member of the college as
soon as ever this wretched
war is over. Meanwhile
keep me informed of
your movements - &
distinctions.
With every good wish
will you kindly
Sincerely Yr..
[underline]L. R. Phelps[/underline]
