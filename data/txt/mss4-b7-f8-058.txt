Waterford 8 Janry 1808
Dear Mary
I have been unavoidably delayed
in making out the enclosed list so that
I can not venture to delay it for another
post the Number of Copies is as I did not
require the Subscriptions as there appeared at
first an uncertainty of publication however
if it should be required I will immediately
call them in & remit - I do not know
the residence of Geo Rothe Esqr but suppose
it to be Co. Kilkenny or Carlow
Excuse me for the present
enlarging
William Rayner

WATERFORD
