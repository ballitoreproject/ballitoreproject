The Outrake,
Little Longstone,
Bakewell.
September 15th. 1896.
Dear Mrs. Davies,
I have the pleasure to send you three copies of our Manchester
Memorial which you may fill up and send direct to the Marquess of
Salisbury, Downing Street, London. I am sure it is a good work to
undertake and GOD will bless you in it.
Yours heartily,
F.W. Crossley
Mrs. Davies,
St. Margarets,
Foxrock. Co. Dublin.
