Cork - Patrick St
1852. July 21st.. Up at
six O'clock this morning &
prepared for journey. Philip
came in before breakfast, so
we were in very good time
for the train which started
at 10 O'clock. Rhoda Stephens
was left in care of the house.
We were trying to prevail on
Ned to come but without success,
As Elizabeth Jane & the children
have gone down to Waterford
John will take his meals
at Britain street - - We
had the carriage to ourselves
all the way to Mallow
where our retirement was
broke in upon by a gentleman.

Cork we were met by a person
