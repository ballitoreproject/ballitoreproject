1st,, Sunday - Margt. better
but still looking pale and
washy - Came out by the [unclear]12[/unclear]
train where I was met by Papa
Ned, Phil, Lyd, and Cha who had
gone out by an earlier train with
Willie Leadbeater - Mary Gough
very kindly went in to spend the
day with Margt. or I do not think
I should have left her at all -
Philip staid at Glendruid - Charlie
went back to town. We drove
round to see Aunt Lydia in her
new lodgings and there were Mary
her husband and two fine little
boys - Mossy a Fisher, Harry a
Fitzmaurice.
