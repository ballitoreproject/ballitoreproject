2nd mo. 1823
My dear E
I gave a false report of the
Packs Top Strang man [insertion] only [/insertion] brought
a letter to thee which Rich d
takes, When the Pack arrives
it shall be taken care of till
call'd for; my mother admires
the robes greatly they are so exceedingly
neat, When thou gets the
diaper send it unhem'd, that
I may have something to do
& send a bill of the muslin thou
got from M Doyle along with
that of the diaper ever thine S B

E Leadbeater
