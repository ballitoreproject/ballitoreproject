~ February ~
1 Friday. N P.
2 Saturday N P.
3 Sunday. Went to meeting (in
the new carriage which is very
snug) with Ned and Philip. Called
at Glendruid where Philip staid.
This was the first day of the late
hour. Charlie came out
but would not stay longer than
after tea.
4 Monday. I went to 4 Glendruid
as I had been invited to do so -
yesterday. It was very pleasant
5 Tuesday. They persuaded me to
stay to day as it was Shrove Tuesday

girls I mean Florence and Jane
