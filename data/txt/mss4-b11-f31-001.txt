My Dear Mrs. Cole
Will you excuse us
this Evening, tho' Kitty
and I promised ourselves,
a very happy Eveng
with you. we are obliged
to remain at home to
see a Cousin of ours
we have not seen for

some time. but any
other Evening that you
are at [underline]leisure[/underline] I need
not say how happy it
would make us,
with kind regards to
Mr Cole
believe me your truly
affect-
Charlotte C Fenton
24th- August 1843
