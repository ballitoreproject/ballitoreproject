11.th Mo 1828
Dear E- hast thou thought about Bath! Duffy has
further call'd on me this evening, he does not wish
her to stay in town so if she can get no place
she is to go to her aunt again next first day
I read thine I think S Haughton has said as
much as she could against Biddy enough
to permit in getting a place if all she
says is to be attended to, she has given
her as good a discharge as M Davis
[unclear]Mainence[/unclear] altho she accused M D
G Downes is in town looking very
fat to & well try Yo

E Leadbeater
I will write again abt Biddy
thou said nought of the other Biddy
