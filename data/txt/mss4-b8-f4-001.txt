Ballitore 19th of 11th mo 1810
My Dear Sister
I hope thou art well. please
answer this letter. Davy
Doyle is recovering. Kitty
Mitchell is very ill.
Molly sends her love,
and hopes that thou art
well. the Fodhunters are
come back, and several other of
the boys Mary Doyle
sends her very dear love to thee
I remain thy affectionate
sister Lydia J L
