10th month
at Dunlavin, by we I mean
Charlie and [underline]one[/underline] or [underline]both[/underline] of us.
So up I had to run and pack
my few things before I went
to bed -
Gt Britain street.
23rd.. A beautiful morning
and on our way to town
soon after ten O'clock.
Went out with Polly and
ordered new bonnets and
bought a pair of boots, were
home just in time for three
O'clock dinner after which we
Started off for the train which
left Dublin at four O'clock.
At quarter to seven we arrived
at Dunlavin where we were
welcomed most heartily.
It was seven years I think
since I had been there, and
