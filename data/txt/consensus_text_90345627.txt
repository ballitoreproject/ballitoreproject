21 Wednesday - Breakfasted at
Aunt Cole's - Margt. came out
with us to see Papa who has not
been well. She staid until
Saturday morning - Papa staid
out all this week -
24 Saturday Charlotte Strangman came
25 Sunday- Papa, Ned Lyd and I
went to meeting -
26 Monday - Phil Lyd and I
dined at Trafalgar Terrace - as
it was Florences birthday and
we were previously invited.
