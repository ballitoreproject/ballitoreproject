Before leaving we went down
to see Derry Vale where Richard
Pike lives and a pretty little place
it is, sheltered by fine trees, and
having a fine grove quite near it.
At last the hour for our departure
arrived and away we drove
waving our hands to our kind
host & hostess who were still standing
at the hall door as we wound
along the avenue -
11 Wednesday - Orange Hill -
This evening we went to James
Bell's to tea - where we met
the Haughtons and a young
French man who accompanied
them. Passed the evening very
pleasantly playing a variety of
amusing games -
