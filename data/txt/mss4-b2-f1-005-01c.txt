
                From the same to the same
Ballitore: 4/9 month 1784
                
My dear James
        ++ The virtue of truth is wonderful- how those that are measurably impregnated by it rejoice in one another's welfare therein! so that it may be said indeed, "What manner of love is this?" And it may likewise be answered "Tis such love as the Father hath loved us withal- therewith we love one another.
