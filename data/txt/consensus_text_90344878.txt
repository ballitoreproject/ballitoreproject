28th.. th First day, up very
early, and drove all sleepiness
away by taking a cold shower
bath. John came out
to the meeting house with
me, then I met Philip
and Polly who had come
in the carriage from Fassaroe
We brought Richard Webb
back also J Perry jr whom
Philip had invited.
called at Glendruid, where
for a wonder we saw Uncle
Manliffe and Uncle Arthur,
Mary Gough was also there
she is staying with Grandmama.
was not at meeting so
we had not the pleasure
of seeing her, which we
all regretted, as we were
looking forward to a little
