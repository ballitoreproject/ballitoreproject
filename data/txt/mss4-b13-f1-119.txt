We were not long at J: Leckey’s when Joshua Beale arrived, not feeling comfortable in going homeward, & a kind, helpful friend we found him. Finished with Jo: Watson after meeting, the 5th, & that evening went to a little Village call’d Myshall, where my devoted friend had a meeting with the poor people there. 6th another meeting of this kind for her at Tullow, I believe they were both satisfactory. Staid over first-day at Killconner side, & on second-day morning set out for Castledermot-side; finished here before their week-day meeting (4th day) except Philip Coleman whom we took in our way (at Castledermot) to Athy, where we arrived same
