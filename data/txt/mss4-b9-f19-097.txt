9th mo 1825
My dear E - I send some clothes for Meg: Nancy
arrived yesterday with the basket for the contents of which
I was much obliged - if the mushrooms are not out of
season I would thank thee to get some for me to
make [unclear]catsup[/unclear] I find I have much less than I
expected - I did not receive your letter till yesterday
the account of poor Mag are very good - I am sorry
that I did not know of Edwd intention of going sooner
or I should have had a message ready for my aunt
Sally tell her so with my love - I shall get the earthenware
for thee as soon as I can thy SB

Elizth
Leadbeater
did the great apples
grow in your garden
