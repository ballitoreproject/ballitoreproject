Fassaroe.
Bray.
20th of Sept 1861
Edward & Huldah Barrington
request the pleasure of
Frances & Lucy Malone's
Company at Fassaroe at
4 o'clock P. M on Wednesday
the 16th of Oct. on the
occasion of their daughter
Selina's marriage -
P. J. B

My dear Lucy
It will give Edward
& I great pleasure if you
can accept of our invitation -
& be with us at the
wedding - My hand is
nearly tired writing notes
though I have good help in
Lydia - It was very pleasant
to [unclear]home[/unclear] even a short hint
from Emily -
Thy afft. Sister
Huldah Barrington
