2nd.. Wednesday. Polly and Margt and
T H went out to Fassaroe by the 8
train this evening - I staid to go to
a concert with Charlie.
3rd.. Thursday. Came out here. Polly
Margt and TH went to Glendruid to tea,
calling at Ballyman on their way.
Papa went to Rathsallagh fair.
4 Friday. Papa and Margt came out
to tea - we are expecting Polly and JH
out to night and to=morrow if fine
we hope to go on some little excursion.
