Moore Park. Octr 21st 1862.
presents his compts to Mrs M. Barrington,
and regrets he is unable to
assist the little Irish orphan boy
who is now a candidate for admission
into the British Orphan Asylum at
Clapham Rise, London, he having
already, at the instance of Miss
Cummins, promised to assist
another orphan.
Mrs Margt Barrington
care of J: Barrington & Sons
Gt Britain St.
Dublin
The Earl of Mount Cashell
