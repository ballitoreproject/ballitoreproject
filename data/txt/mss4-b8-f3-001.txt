12th mo: 1828
My dear Elizabeth
If thou should receive an account
from Dublin tomorrow I would be
much obliged if thou [insertion]would[/insertion] send word to Eliza
by the messenger before he brings it rather
as she will be anxious to hear. also if there
is a letter for her [deletion][/deletion] please to [deletion]o[/deletion] send it
to her that she may read it and send
it out to me, I suppose thou knows
she is at Betsy Barringtons, I would
not be so particular dare that I forget
to tell thee to day when I saw
thee.
thiyne sincerely Selina
Moone 4th day night

Elizabeth Leadbeater
Ballitore
