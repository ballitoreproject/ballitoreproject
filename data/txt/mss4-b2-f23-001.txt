Clonmel 12th month 24th 1788.

Dear Grandfather,

	As this is the first time I ever wrote to thee, I hope it will be agreeable to thee, hoping that thou wilt not be disple^a^sed at my long silence. We have a meeting every third day evening, we have got 16 scholars, I am learning Geography, I like ^ to be here ^ very well, it is very pleasant. My Father, Mother, & Uncle Robert, Aunt Sarah, R. Grubb, Cousin Elizabeth Luke, Anne Broadhead, Sarah Alexander, Ellen Fayle, Hannah Grubb, Elizabeth Jacob, Brother Richard & Sister Sally, join me with dear love to you all yours and uncles Family, farewell dear Grandfather, and believe me to be thy affecti^onate^ Grandaughter.

Anne Grubb
