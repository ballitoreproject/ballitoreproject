23d. a little after 6
My dear Eliz
I have just seen A Doyle after a
fine night - she sends her dear Love bids
me say she is finely & hopes you will meet
again. I am scribbling without specs &
undressed to tell thee this, & to send thee
the enclosed which handing to
GBewley when he was here before
[unclear]he[/unclear] seemed to think high & I engaged to
ask thee & forgot till last night when
he again enquired: So thou can charge
them what is proper when GD & thee
meet in Mt melick
thy ML
I have sent another letter by
[unclear]Zanny[/unclear]

Eliz Leadbeater
2d letter
Edenderry
