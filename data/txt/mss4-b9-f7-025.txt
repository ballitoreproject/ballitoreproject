My dear Elizabeth
I had written the enclosed
note to Edw McEvoy this morning
in reply to one from him. On second
thoughts I much prefer sending it to
you to open and read to him as tho'
it was one to yourself. He seems
to me to be [insertion]a[/insertion] man whom it is very
difficult to pin upon any particular point.
He offers four guineas per Ann for the
[unclear][/unclear] you will percieve the rest
from my note to him. I remain
Yours affectionately
EB
Oct 31th 1838
I wish you a pleasant Halloween

Elizabeth Leadbeater
