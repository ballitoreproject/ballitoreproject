6th.. J and I went over
to William street this
morning to invite Arthur
to come and spend next
fourth day evening
with us. and then
went to buy a few
things Josephine wanted.
Margt having a
cold this morning
staid in bed until
after breakfast. Papa
had come in when
we returned by no Polly,
only a letter from her,
saying she thought
it better not to mind
coming in now -
Lydia, Maria, & TH White
