6th mo 1828
My dear E, thou hast old trouble with my affairs, I
want to know more about the towels is the Diaper blend I
got it should be exactly the same as what thou got from
the association for thy table clothes I would wish to get
a doz of them thou would know the right length
to have them wide proportion'd - also would thou
take the trouble to send the parcel Edw takes, to my name
it is for Ally's little girl, did my nurse go to thee for
Money? I hope to see thou soon ever thy S B

E Leadbeater
Ballitore
Recevied 4th of
6th mo 1828
