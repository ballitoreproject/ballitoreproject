Dublin 8th of 5th month 1804.
Dear William
I received thy favour yesterday with
the halves of Bank Notes as stated in thy letter, I should
have acknowledged the receipt of them by yesterday's Post
but was unavoidably prevented, however I expect thou hast
not been uneasy on that account - will thou please to [unclear]/unclear]
by the next Cover whether I shall send the note by the
following Post - I remain thy affect. frd.
William Gatchell

Forage

William Leadbeater
Ballitore
