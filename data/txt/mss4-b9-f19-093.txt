1825
My dear E
I have just heard that Thos Webb
goes to Ballitore early in the week
with his brother, I send the coller which
came wrapp'd round the goose, to Deb,
along with some hair for herself thou
canst ask her for it, we spent last
week at Glendruid where Margt. was
very poorly for 3 days but is recover'd
again Johnny is very well but still
toothless - D Johonson able to be down
stairs, I am in great haste as I keep
Bidd waiting to take this I hope you
are all well
I got the old
coat to day
29th
thy SB

Elizth. Leadbeater
Ballitore
