May 1823 filing note
My Dear Mrs Leadbeater
I know that you are so much interested in the
cause of humanity that I cannot forbear sending the enclosed
I am sincerely happy to hear of your welfare and of that of your family
and I am your sincere friend
Frances Smith
we were extremely obliged by your communication
relation to poor Ireland- the facts were dreadful, but exciting
Mr Smith was glad to be informed that he might do what he could
I am truly glad or Mr [unclear]Ferris[/unclear]
prosperity
May 25 1823

Ongar twenty fifth May 1823
Mrs Leadbetter
Ballitore
Ireland
