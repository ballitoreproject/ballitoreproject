6th mo 1826
My dear E --- I had a pleasant letter from Lydia
& Jas L was able to walk to town &
had come to her own looks & feeling
again I am in gt haste going to the Glen
I hope thou liked the earthenware the
bottom of the same tureen cost 6d I gave
him the lid & 3d pence which paid him
all in the basket which I sent by George's man [insertion]cost 3/10[/insertion]

please give this shammy skin to Betsy
it cost 8d thy SB
