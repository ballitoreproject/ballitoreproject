This is William Pike's wife and
a very agreeable amiable woman
she is. After tea which was
very acceptable after our long drive
we went out to the garden
which is very nicely kept, and
the greenhouse which is an
elegant one and adorned with
most choice and handsome
flowers. The fuschias and
Calceolariars were exceedingly
beautiful and there were also
some fine geraniums.
It was such a fine evening
