108 Just before we left the house, dear Nancy, like an obedient child as she is, did as I believe she was rightly bidden, & we came away I hope in peace, Fifth-evening we went to Rathcool, several friends from Dublin with us. We took leave of our dear Job, it being uncertain whether we proceeded Southward, or returned: it was a sweet leave, & taking hold of my hand (as my dear father used) he dropped a few encouraging words into my heart, - the words are gone, but I think I still feel the power.
