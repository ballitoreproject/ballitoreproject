Dear Friend;
	As I find thou art going to Leck I just request you wilt call at Hare Gate. I have an Aunt lives there. I am sure will be ple^a^sed to see thee. I wish thee a satisfactory jurney [sic] and safe return to us again  My Dear love to all. And am thy affectionate Friend S. Appleby
excuse hast [sic]
Hare Gate is but a little Mile from Leck
