After dinner Polly Avy Lid
and I went up to the hay
field where we sat reading
working and romping
about. It was a most
delightful evening and
Polly and I staid until
the bell rang, long after
the children had left us.
Poll reading Shenstones
poems to me while I worked
at my stag.
28th.. Spent a delightful
day in the Dargle -
a juvenile pic-nic for
Avy and Liddy. Charlotte
and the little Pims, Margt.
