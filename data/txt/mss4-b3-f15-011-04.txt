Ballitore 2/9 month 1782

My dear friend
	I would not regret my Friends [Edmund Burke] being out of place if his own interest and the public good did not suffer by it. It does not appear to me to be an eligible post; to be stationed at the helm in such a time as this. Qualities of capitol [sic] magnitude are necessary to statement in the present crisis and qualifications of a particular kind. The sound reason, philosophy & integrity of Edmund Burke joined with the political talents of others might very much serve the state, but
